# 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

**最后更新：2024年12月**

## 简介

Card Tab（卡片导航）是一款浏览器新标签页扩展，让您自定义和组织常用网站链接。

## 数据收集

**我们不收集任何个人数据。**

本扩展：
- ❌ 不收集用户信息
- ❌ 不跟踪浏览历史
- ❌ 不使用分析服务
- ❌ 不与第三方共享数据

## 数据存储

所有数据（分类、链接、设置）均存储在您的浏览器本地存储（`chrome.storage.local`）中：
- 数据仅保存在您的设备上
- 不会上传到任何服务器
- 您可以随时通过扩展设置导出或删除数据

## 权限说明

本扩展请求以下权限：
- **storage**：用于保存您的链接和设置配置

## 网络请求

本扩展仅在以下情况发起网络请求：
- 获取网站图标（通过 Google Favicon 服务）

## 联系方式

如有问题或建议，请通过 GitHub Issues 联系：
https://github.com/xixia123/HomeTabPage/issues

# Privacy Policy (English)

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Introduction

Card Tab is a browser new tab extension that allows you to customize and organize your frequently used website links.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

This extension:
- ❌ Does not collect user information
- ❌ Does not track browsing history
- ❌ Does not use analytics services
- ❌ Does not share data with third parties

## Data Storage

All data (categories, links, settings) is stored locally in your browser (`chrome.storage.local`):
- Data is only saved on your device
- It is never uploaded to any server
- You can export or delete your data at any time through the extension settings

## Permissions

This extension requests the following permissions:
- **storage**: To save your links and settings

## Network Requests

This extension only makes network requests to:
- Fetch website favicons (via Google Favicon service)

## Contact

For questions or suggestions, please contact us via GitHub Issues:
https://github.com/xixia123/HomeTabPage/issues
